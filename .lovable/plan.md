

## DovesMind Synergy - Homepage Implementation Plan

### Overview
A modern, calming, and professional psychological support & substance abuse prevention website homepage for DovesMind Synergy, designed for the Nigerian audience. The design will follow the premium mental-health aesthetic from your reference image.

---

### Visual Design System

**Color Palette:**
- Primary background: Deep teal/dark green (#0D2818, #1A3C34)
- Accent: Soft lime/mint green (#A3E635, #BBF451)
- Light sections: White (#FFFFFF) and soft gray (#F8FAFC)
- Text: Dark foreground for light sections, white for dark sections

**Design Elements:**
- Rounded cards with soft shadows
- Curved section dividers (wave shapes between sections)
- Subtle gradients and abstract organic blob shapes
- Modern sans-serif typography with generous spacing
- Accessibility-friendly contrast ratios

---

### Homepage Sections

#### 1. Header Navigation
- DovesMind Synergy logo (left)
- Navigation links: Home, About, Services, Training Programs, Team, Contact
- Primary CTA button: "Book Consultation" (lime green)

- **Headline:** "Professional Psychological Support & Substance Abuse Prevention"

  1. Confidential & Ethical Support
  2. Professional Psychological Guidance
  3. Trusted Rehabilitation Referrals

  6. Stronger Families & Communities

---

### Technical Implementation

**Frontend:**
- React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
- Shadcn/ui components for forms, tabs, cards
- Smooth scroll animations and transitions
- Fully responsive (mobile-first design)
- WCAG accessibility standards

**Forms:**
- Client-side validation using zod
- Success toast notifications on submission
- Form data collected in state (no backend yet)

**Placeholder Elements:**
- Placeholder images with African-diverse styling notes
- "Coming Soon" states where needed
- Easy content replacement later

---

### Deliverables
A complete, polished single-page homepage that:
- Establishes trust and professionalism
- Clearly communicates DovesMind Synergy's mission and services
- Provides working contact form (frontend validation, success feedback)
- Is ready for content updates and backend integration later
